Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a physical shop?

No, we do not have a physical storefront. We are a home-based artisan business. All orders are handled personally via WhatsApp or phone.

How do I pick up my order?

We arrange **in-person pick-ups** for all orders. Once your gift is ready, we will coordinate a convenient time and location with you.

Can I customize a hamper?

Absolutely! Customization is our specialty. Let us know your budget and preferences, and we will curate something special.
